# Totally Not Real Energy Drinks (Fabric)

**Totally Not Real Energy Drinks** is a small Fabric mod that adds reusable drink cans and a handful of “totally not real” sodas & energy drinks brewed in a Brewing Stand.

Instead of flooding your inventory with glass bottles, drinks come in stackable cans and return an **Empty Can** after drinking.

Hold an **Empty Can** and right click a **water-filled cauldron**.

*   Consumes 1 cauldron level
*   Gives Water Can

## Brewing Recipes (All Drinks)

Brewing stands take **one ingredient at a time**, so each drink is brewed in **3 steps**:All drinks:  
\- are **drinkable**  
\- **stack to 16**  
\- **return an Empty Can** after drinking
